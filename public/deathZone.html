Death Zone

  • This page displays the death zones in the game.

  • The canvas below shows the areas where players die the most.

  • The color intensity indicates the frequency of deaths:

    • Black means no death

    • Light red means fewer deaths.

    • Dark red means more deaths.

  • Hover over a square to see the exact number of deaths in that area.
