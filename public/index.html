Solid Snake

Multiplayer Online Snake Game

How To Play

Game Basics: Control your snake with ⬆ ⬇ ⬅ ⮕. Eat fruits to grow and avoid other players. Press Z to boost speed.

Fruit System: Regular fruits increase length and score. Special fruits provide unique abilities.

Weather System: Dynamic weather affects gameplay. Adapt to conditions like rain and snow.

Ranking

Compete against the world's top players and climb the rankings in various categories such as score, level, and kills. Test your skills and strive to be the best in each category. The leaderboard updates regularly, so keep playing and improve your ranking to become the ultimate champion!

Analytics

Review your game statistics, including level, total score, average score per game, highest score per game, total kills, K/D ratio, most used skin, and more.
